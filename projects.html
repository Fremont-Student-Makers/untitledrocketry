Our Work

American Rocketry Challenge

2022-2023 Season

We designed and built a rocket to safely carry a raw egg to a specific altitude and return it to the ground undamaged within a set time frame. This involved multiple design iterations, 3D printed parts, and rigorous testing to meet the strict competition requirements.

High Power Rocketry

Ongoing

We are continuously pushing the boundaries of our rocketry skills by designing, building, and launching high-power rockets. This involves complex simulations, advanced materials, and obtaining the necessary certifications to fly these rockets thousands of feet into the air.

Modular Rockets

2023-Present

To increase our launch cadence and adaptability, we developed a fully modular rocket system. All major components are 3D printed and designed to be easily swappable, allowing us to customize rocket configurations for different payload weights and flight conditions on the fly.
