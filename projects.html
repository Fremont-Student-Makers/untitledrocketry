Our Work

American Rocketry Challenge

Ongoing

We have been competing in the American Rocketry Challenge for multiple seasons, designing and building rockets to safely carry payloads to specific altitudes and return them undamaged within set time frames. The majority of our team members are currently pursuing their Level 1 and Level 2 high-power rocketry certifications as part of this initiative.

High Power Rocketry

Ongoing

We are continuously pushing the boundaries of our rocketry skills by designing, building, and launching high-power rockets. This involves complex simulations, advanced materials, and obtaining the necessary certifications to fly these rockets thousands of feet into the air.

Modular Rockets

2023-Present

To increase our launch cadence and adaptability, we developed a fully modular rocket system. All major components are 3D printed and designed to be easily swappable, allowing us to customize rocket configurations for different payload weights and flight conditions on the fly.

Supersonic Flight Initiative

In Development

We are ambitiously pursuing the design and launch of a rocket capable of breaking the sound barrier. This cutting-edge project requires advanced aerodynamic modeling, high-thrust motor systems, and meticulous engineering to achieve supersonic velocities while maintaining structural integrity and safe recovery.
