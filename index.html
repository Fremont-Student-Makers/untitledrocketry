About Us

Mission Statement: At Untitled Rocketry, our mission is to push the limits of amateur aerospace engineering through collaborative innovation, rigorous testing, and a passion for exploration. We aim to inspire the next generation of engineers by sharing our knowledge and achievements with the community.

Founded by a group of high-school enthusiasts, we design, build, and launch high-performance rockets, combining cutting-edge materials and precise simulation tools to achieve new altitudes and milestones.
